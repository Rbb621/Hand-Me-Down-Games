About Me

I’m Andy Clarke, a man who loves video games and wants to create the video games I want to play.
My goal is to create easy to understand video games that are accessible, achieve depth, and are hard to master at the same time. I have been learning how to create video games since I was 14 years old. I have experience with companies like Emagination Computer Camps, Game Gen, and Whim Indie. I am working on creating video games as my dream career, and I can’t wait to see where this path leads. Keep moving forward!

Color Dash is a video game where you must match the color of the ground to survive. It’s a 2.5D autorunner platformer game, and the goal is to survive as long as you can.

There are four colors; red, blue, yellow, and green. Colorblind options are also available!

Color Dash is fun for everyone, whether you’re a beginner to video games or are a seasoned pro at video games.
